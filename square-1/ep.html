Edge Permutation

Printable Version

These EP algs are from Lars Vandenbergh's site, this thread, and from videos on David Woner's and Bingliang Li's YouTube channels.

Red text indicates a case with parity, and an asterisk (*) indicates that the equator will be flipped after the alg is performed.

The first digit indicates the cycle on the D layer and the second digit indicates the cycle on the U layer. The numbering system is as followed:

0: all edges permuted, 1: adjacent swap, 2: opposite swap, 3: clockwise U perm, 4: counterclockwise U perm, 5: Z perm, 6: H perm, 7: clockwise O perm, 8: counterclockwise O perm, 9: W perm.

All U or D moves before an alg are AUF/ADFs; they're not part of the alg.
